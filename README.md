## 故事背景 Story Overview

游戏的主角是一名在心理咨询诊所实习的学生。这家诊所具有特殊的治疗方式——可以通过进入患者的梦境来进行心理治疗
The story follows the protagonist, a psychology intern working at an unusual counselling clinic. This clinic has a unique method of therapy: it allows therapists to enter a patient’s dreams in order to treat psychological trauma.

在治疗过程中，主角会进入患者的梦境世界，走入其记忆深处。玩家需要在这些梦境环境中进行探索，解开象征性的障碍，并面对患者内心深处的恐惧与情绪投射，帮助患者逐步敞开心扉、完成心理疗愈。
As part of the treatment process, the protagonist enters the dreams of patients and walks through their memories. Within these dream worlds, the player explores symbolic environments, solves challenges, and confronts manifestations of the patients’ inner fears and suppressed emotions. Through these interactions, the player helps patients gradually open up and heal.

游戏的叙事核心围绕记忆、情绪与自我接纳展开，通过梦境式的关卡设计来映射不同患者的心理状态。
The narrative focuses on themes such as memory, emotion, and self-acceptance, using dream-like environments to reflect the psychological state of each patient.

## Gameplay Features
- Dream-based exploration
- Narrative-driven quests
- Enemy AI with patrol / chase / attack states
- Third-person camera system
